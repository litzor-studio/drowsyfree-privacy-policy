DrowsyFree

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

DrowsyFree (the "App") takes your privacy seriously and operates its privacy policy as described below.

1. Information We Collect

📷 Camera Access

Used solely for drowsiness detection through face and eye recognition. Camera footage is not transmitted to servers and is processed in real time on-device, then immediately discarded.

📍 GPS Location

Used for vehicle speed display and driving/stopping detection. Location data is processed in real time on-device and is not stored or transmitted to servers.

🔑 Google Account Information

When using the social login feature, your Google account email and profile photo are stored in Firebase Authentication.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

• Facial photos or biometric data (no server transfer/storage) • Contacts, messages, or call history • Driving routes or movement records

3. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used only for the following purposes: • Drowsiness, yawning, and distraction detection and alerts • GPS-based vehicle speed display and driving/stopping state detection • Firebase error analysis (Crashlytics) — only de-identified app error information is collected

4. Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

Authentication, Crashlytics, Analytics, and Remote Config are used. Google's Privacy Policy applies.

Google ML Kit

Face recognition is processed entirely on-device. It is not transmitted to external servers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

• Settings data: Automatically deleted when app is uninstalled • Firebase account: Deletable via in-app account deletion or Firebase Console

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 14. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children.

7. Privacy Officer

Email: ad.pyeon@gmail.com For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at the above email.

8. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, you will be notified through app updates and in-app announcements.

⚠️ This app is an assistive tool that detects drowsiness while driving and provides alerts. We do not guarantee 100% detection of all drowsiness situations. The driver bears final responsibility for safe driving.
